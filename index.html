Welcome to my website! I am a PhD candidate at the Paris School of Economics and a research fellow at the World Inequality Lab. I am also a faculty lecturer at the faculty of economics, Thammasat University since 2020. I am originally from Bangkok, Thailand.

My research focuses on economic history of Southeast Asian economies as well as historical evolution of economic inequality. You can find my CV here. A list of my publications can be found here.


Feel free to contact me at thanasak.jenmana[at]psemail.eu and jenmana[at]econ.tu.ac.th.
